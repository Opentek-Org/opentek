Who are we and What do we do?

If it about Open Source, it is on Opentek

Welcome to Opentek, your number one source for Open Source Development. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of tools, resources, and projects, with a focus on Collaboration, Code, and Networking.

The problems we are trying to solve:

  • No need of excessive Google search to find events, programs or resources.
  • This organization is completely devoted to Open Source and Networking.
  • Language specific projects where an absolute beginner can also contribute.
  • Coding and Collaborating in an inclusive environment

This community is going to help accelerate students' learning, and bring them closer to like-minded individuals, who could all be a valuable asset in their journey towards a better future in technology.
